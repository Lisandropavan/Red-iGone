What is Red iGone? Red iGone is simply the easiest way to remove red eyes from photos. In three simple steps and a matter of seconds, you can turn an image ruined by red eyes into a fantastic looking photo.

In addition to this nifty application for your iPhone, Red iGone is also available as a web app. This means that you can just point the web browser to RediGone.com and remove red eyes directly on your PC or Mac.

Who's behind Red iGone? The company behind Red iGone is called Devify, a young swedish technology company. For more information about Devify, please visit Devify.com.
